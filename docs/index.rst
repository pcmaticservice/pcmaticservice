How to Submit a Support Ticket
==============================

To create a support request:

1. Visit the official PC Matic support website.
2. Open the technical support section.
3. Enter your name and email address.
4. Include your order information if available.
5. Describe your issue in detail.
6. Submit the request.
7. Check your email for a confirmation message and future updates. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}
